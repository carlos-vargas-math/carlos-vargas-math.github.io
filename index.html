Welcome to Carlos Vargas' personal webpage

I obtained my PhD in Mathematics in 2015 from Universitaet des Saarlandes, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Roland Speicher.

I am interested in free and (operator-valued) non-commutative probability, large random matrices, incidence algebras, cumulants and statistics, posets and their combinatorial Moebius functions.
